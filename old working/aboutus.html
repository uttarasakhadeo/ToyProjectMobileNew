Toy Mobile Project

Glimmpse Lite was developed by Uttara Sakhadeo, Drs. Aarti Munjal and Sarah Kreidler.

GLIMMPSE Lite is a part of a large research project to provide researchers such as behavioral and social scientists with tools and education related to the following aspects of study design:
  1. Calculating power and sample size for any univariate or multivariate test for the general linear multivariate model, assuming fixed predictors.
  2. Producing confidence intervals on power estimates for designs with fixed predictors.
  3. Producing power calculations for designs with a single Gaussian covariate.
  4. Supporting designs with unequal group sizes and complicated covariance structures.
  5. Creating basic power curves.

Additional documentation, publications and related software products are available from http://samplesizeshop.org
